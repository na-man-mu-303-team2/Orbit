# Brandlogy Modern Design Pack

Use this as a reusable design system, not as visible slide content.

## Core Tone
- Modern Korean startup presentation with polished consulting-deck density.
- White canvas, strong hierarchy, high whitespace discipline, and confident data-first composition.
- Primary color is Brandlogy blue, secondary accent is vivid pink. Use pink sparingly for callouts, chips, and short emphasis.
- Use Pretendard for headings and body text. Keep typography compact, clean, and readable.

## Layout Rules
- 16:9 wide slide, 1920x1080 logical canvas.
- Keep content inside a safe area of roughly x=120, y=96, width=1680, height=888.
- Cover slides should make the topic unmistakable in the first viewport: large left title, concise subtitle, right-side abstract visual or simple diagram.
- Body slides should prefer chart-first, table-first, process, comparison, or metric-card layouts over plain bullet lists.
- Use no fake logos in phase 1. If a Brandlogy mark is required later, leave space only.

## Content Density
- For presentation mode, keep slide body concise and move details into speaker notes.
- For report-like decks, use dense but structured blocks: cards, tables, charts, and short evidence captions.
- Avoid decorative clutter. Every shape should carry structure, emphasis, or grouping.

## Visual Details
- Use an 8px radius or less for cards and controls.
- Use thin borders and restrained shadows only when grouping content.
- Prefer simple geometric blocks, key numbers, and diagrams. Do not use blurred orb or gradient-blob decoration.
